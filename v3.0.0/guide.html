User’s Guide

Before you start diving into the details of programming systems and blocks, have a look at the mathematical background of dynamical systems, if you haven’t already. That theory will help you understand much better what goes on in the system simulation.

Also, as MoDyPy is heavily based on NumPy, you might want check out their documentation as well, at least the basic introduction into N-dimensional arrays. However, you can also pick up a lot of that along the way.
